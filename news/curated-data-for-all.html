General Awesomeness Approaching

Curated Data For All

Users are now able to see some data, averaged mostly.

News sentiment is whether the latest news were positive or negative.

Median price guess is the median guesses of all users and technical analysis interpretations.

Social sentiment is what people say on the internet regarding bitcoin price.

TA views is kind of like the technical analysis sentiment and its impact combined.

Positive values good, negatives bad. Values go from -100% to +100%.

We'll be sharing a lot. Also want our users to share their sentiments.

So our motto nowadays is study often, post often, earn together, share the wealth.

More features
 

We created a page to match the volatility with news. Obviously this fails when there is no news or the volatility is not related to news. We'll improve this feat in the future.

In-app notifications: On top of the page you'll see red notifications when a sudden price move happens or when a big news emerge.

You'll see a lot of news coming as tasks towards you. Also we created an AI to act fast and filter some news before humans touch. Final word still belongs to humans.

Important tasks like interpretation of immediate news effects to price, will be thrown at you first. 
 

Earning Coins is Back


You'll earn coins for all the tasks you completed. You'll also see an approximate USD value for the tasks. You can regard this USD value as what Guess.Market values your work at current rates. As our project is new we still in the process of valuation of our coins. It might be less value in the future or it might be more valuable. We'll see. So that USD number is what we are willing to pay for the work. You can ask us to pay you in coins after it reaches a considerable amount. We'll investigate your account and hopefully if everything is clear we'll pay you in coins.
