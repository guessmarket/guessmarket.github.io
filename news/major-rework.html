Two steps forward 1 steps back

Major Client Rework

You are seeing the new Guess.Market app on a webpage!

I am rewriting the app from scratch using Ionic. Now we will be able to access the system both on mobile and desktop.

The reasons for this move:

1. We needed desktop web client because it is impossible to interpret technical analysis on mobile
2. It would be painful to manage 3 projects for 3 platforms
3. The app needed a major rework anyway while moving to a tasks based design.

The work in progress version here: http://app.guess.market

Currently there are no push notifications. The notifications are only within the app, shown as red headers.

TA Interpretation
 

Bitcoin price follows TA rules among other things. It is a combination of sentiment of each participant in the market, but technical analysis surely plays a big role.

Human brain loves patterns.  Once enough people believe in TA, it becomes a reality. Like self fulfilling prophecy.

We always want to understand why traders and investors behave in the way they behave, so we'll study TA. Interpreting and conversion of TA in to actionable trading singals is one of the things we will do.
 

Volatility Detector Arrived


It is a pain to watch a price that never moves. What if there was a service that alerts you when there is volatility.

We made that a reality.

This volatility detector algorithm looks at volume and price ranks with different time horizons and tries to detect when something unusual is going on with the price.

Transferring coins from legacy apps


Because we are switching to Ionic based apps, the previous accounts won't be reachable for a while.
If you want to transfer your coins from existing iOS and Android apps, let us know.
