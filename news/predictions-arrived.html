Bulls, bears and numbers

New Gadgets to Play With

The aggregate sentiment of what Guess.Market thinks will be shown like this picture. Isn't it awesome?

The animals will move based on the magnitude of the overall site-wide sentiment/data.. Bear throwing bull out of the ring when the aggregate sentiment is -37%.

Actual predictions
 

In case you wondered what are we going to do with all these data, we started generating predictions and also how certain they are. You will see them like:

 
A model based on social sentiment predicts -6 to +6 percent move in the next 48 hours and another model based on TA views is predicting -5 to +5 percent move. Check out the app for live signals.

These are time-tested signals! The biggest problem with machine learning in finance is the models can't adapt to regime. Awesome models that generate huge profits just start losing when something happens like going from bull to bear or low volatility regime to high volatility regime. 

We tested these models for a year and now you are seeing the final signals ready to be consumed.

We currently predict prices in the next 48 hours. We also provide confidence intervals.

We will try providing several predictions so you'll be able to get a median of all. Median seems to be always working with machine learning and human generated data as well.

Tweet volume and Search volume


Bitcoin still lacks adoption. It is going to be a long ride until the world runs on bitcoin.

So the mere volume of tweets and searches mean a lot. I.e. if there is a hype, then people will search bitcoin.

We started showing these stats to give an idea to our users. We'll try to convert these to predictions as well.

More Bullish Than Ever


Yesterday one of the companies in quant/blockchain space, Numerai, announced that they are building Erasure. This is like decentralized version of Guess.Market. This is both good and bad news. We have a distant competitor but this also proves that the idea is explored by others (with previous execution track records and investment by awesome ventures and people).
