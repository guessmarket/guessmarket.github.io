Getting physical more than ever

Introducing:
Swipe Right

Ever swiped news? You heard that right. We are swiping news from now on!

Wait, how? You think this is important news, swipe right.  You think this is noise, or can't really see how this will change the price. Swipe left.

Swiping will make it more fun to discover news and enable discovering them faster. The news that get more and more 'HOT' votes will eventually be 'added' to the app and pushed to all users. 

For now it will be a feature in iOS app.

Below  you can see the distribution of votes for news, positive values mean bullish, negative means bearish.

As you can see we have a huge bias towards being bullish. Notice the big pile around 1.0. This strategy (permabull) would stunningly work in 2017. But we are in 2018. And we have to be more realistic. Our further research should be around how to systematically balance bullish votes with bearish ones using machine learning.
